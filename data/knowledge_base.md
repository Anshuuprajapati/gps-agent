Q: Aapki support team kitne baje available hai?
A: Hamari support team subah 9 baje se raat 9 baje tak available hai, saatho din.

Q: GPS device kaise kaam karta hai?
A: GPS device vehicle ki location, battery status, aur power connection ki jaankari har kuch minute mein hamare server ko bhejta hai. Agar yeh data band ho jaaye, hum aapko turant contact karte hain.

Q: Service ka charge kitna hai?
A: Service ka charge vehicle ke issue aur location par depend karta hai. Exact quote ke liye humari billing team aapko call karke bataayegi.

Q: Complaint/ticket ka status kaise check karein?
A: Aap apna ticket ID hume bhejein, hum turant uska current status bata denge.

Q: Engineer kab tak pahunchega?
A: Engineer assign hone ke baad, aam taur par 24-48 ghante ke andar site par pahunchte hain, location ke hisaab se.

Q: Kya main apni booking cancel ya reschedule kar sakta hoon?
A: Haan, aap apna naya preferred date/time bata sakte hain, hum booking update kar denge.

Q: GPS device kharab ho jaaye toh replacement free hai kya?
A: Agar device warranty period mein hai toh replacement free hai. Warran
ty ke bina, replacement charge lagega jo humari team confirm karegi.

Q: Aapse contact kaise karein agar koi naya issue ho?
A: Aap isi number par WhatsApp kar sakte hain ya humari support team ko call kar sakte hain — dono available hain.
